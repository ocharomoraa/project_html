About Meraki Agri-tech

Meraki Agritech is a start up agritech company which aims at providing solutions to the challenges Kenyan and Rwandan farmers face. This is achieved by providing them with a platform where they can sell their farm products and acquire loans and services at a faster and affordable rates. Below are the key principles of Meraki Agritech:



Farmer's Mind

In Meraki Agritech we think like farmers. Being acquainted with the farmer's work and aware of the challenges that he faces, we attempt to provide products and service suited to specific working environments. Haifa teams worldwide maintain continuous contact with growers, hearing from them about their needs, in order to share our knowledge and provide them solutions that consider their preferences.

sustainability

As a company that advances agriculture worldwide, Meraki Agritech puts the environment at the forefront of its concerns, attempting to minimize the ecological footprint of all our operations and products. For its deep connection to the environment, Meraki Agritech has undertaken the challenge of being part of global efforts and to act in accordance with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals.

Increasing agricultural productivity

Modern agriculture is required to feed the growing world population, using depleting resources of land, water and minerals. Haifa Group is committed to provide breakthrough developments to allow maximum efficiency of crop production, to provide more food with optimal conditions.

Pioneering the future

The key mission of Meraki Agritech is to create efficient and effective plant-feeding solutions and tools for farmers around the world. We constantly create inspirational solutions for the growing challenges of our changing world, and provide innovative products designed to enhance plant nutrition and boost grower's profit while minimizing environmental harm
